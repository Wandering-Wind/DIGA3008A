Games

Mahala & Autumn

Mahala & Autumn is a 3D narrative game where the player is a lost cat and has to find it's way back ot it's owner. The environment is littered with clues on which way to get home, and how you got lost! A team effort done by Meghan Rogers, Arya Pillay, and myself! Arya being the lead artist, Meghan being the UI/UX designer and level designer and myself as the lead programmer (as well as shader graph artist).

Escaping Cronus

Escaping Cronus is a 2D top down two-player tile game. Players have limited moves each time, with a time ticking to get to the centre first! A team effort between Aiden Africa, Jaiden Muruvan, Unathi Mbatha and myself! Unathi being our artist and the rest of us as programmers!
